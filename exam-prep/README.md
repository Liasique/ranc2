## How to Use This Repository

1. **Organize by Subject**: Add your subjects in the `subjects/` directory
2. **Create Study Guides**: Develop comprehensive study guides in `study-guides/`
3. **Practice Regularly**: Add and solve practice questions in `practice-questions/`
4. **Quick Revision**: Create concise revision notes in `revision-notes/`
5. **Plan Your Time**: Use the `schedule/` directory to track your study plan

## Getting Started

1. List your exam subjects in `subjects/subjects-list.md`
2. Create a study schedule in `schedule/study-schedule.md`
3. Add study materials for each subject
4. Practice with questions regularly
5. Review your revision notes before exams

## Tips for Effective Exam Preparation

- **Start Early**: Begin preparation well in advance
- **Be Consistent**: Study regularly rather than cramming
- **Practice Active Recall**: Test yourself frequently
- **Take Breaks**: Use the Pomodoro technique (25min study, 5min break)
- **Stay Organized**: Keep your notes and materials well-structured
- **Review Regularly**: Revisit topics to reinforce learning
